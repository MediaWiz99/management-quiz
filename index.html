Instructions for Students

Welcome to your first step in discovering what kind of manager you could become! This quiz will help you understand your natural management tendencies, communication style, and leadership preferences. There are no "right" or "wrong" answers - just honest insights into your personality.


How to Take the Quiz:

  • Don't overthink - go with your first instinct
  • Be honest - this is about discovering YOUR authentic style
